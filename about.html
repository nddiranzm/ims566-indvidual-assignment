about us

welcome to our pages

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) is the largest comprehensive university in Malaysia, with 34 campuses, 14 faculties, 9 academic centers, and over 500 programs ranging from Foundation to PhD levels, including professional programs. UiTM offers innovative education with advanced infrastructure and technology, playing a vital role in nation-building and nurturing the potential of future generations.

UiTM is ranked 587th in the QS World University Rankings 2025, 101st in the QS Asia University Rankings 2025, and 107th in the UI GreenMetric World University Rankings 2024. It has also excelled in the QS World University Rankings by Subject 2024, with Hospitality & Leisure Management ranked among the top 50-100 globally. Additionally, UiTM holds the 136th position in the Times Higher Education Interdisciplinary Science Rankings 2025.

UiTM has earned prestigious accolades, including the 3G SDGs Championship Award 2022, the Reader’s Digest Trusted Brand Gold Award for 12 consecutive years, and The BrandLaureate BestBrands Awards 2022 – The Nation’s Pride Higher Education. With over 770,000 alumni across various fields, UiTM aspires to become a world-class university by 2025.

Profile of Director

His Majesty Sultan Ibrahim King of Malaysia

Position: University Counselor

His Majesty the Yang di-Pertuan Agong is the Head of the University. DYMM Tuanku Chancellor determines the frequency and date for convocation and he is the one who chairs it. DYMM Tuanku Chancellor awards degrees, diplomas, certificates and other academic achievements to anyone who has undergone studies approved by the University. DYMM Tuanku Chancellor also awarded an honorary degree or any other award as suggested by the Board of Directors after discussion with the Senate. DYMM Tuanku Chancellor also performs other duties as stipulated in the MARA University of Technology Act 1976 (Act 173).

Her Royal Highness Tengku Permaisuri Hajah Norashikin

Position: Pro-Counselor

Her Royal Highness Tengku Permaisuri Hajah Norashikin is the Pro-chancellor of Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), and was declared on June 14, 2022, starting from October 18, 2021 to October 17, 2024.
